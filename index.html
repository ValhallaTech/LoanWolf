About

LoanWolf is a calculator that will do basic calculations of common loan types such as mortgages, auto loans, student loans, or personal loans. It was started as a class project during the CoderFoundry bootcamp Week 3. The students involved are Kenan Bjelosevic, Adrian Edelen and Fred Smith. The results you currently see are part of a weekend challenge that the team worked together to create.
